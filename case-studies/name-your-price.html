Client MGG Name Your Price

Project type Website Design

Release 2023

The aim AustinShow was looking to relaunch NYP together with new partners in Misfits Gaming Group, with a series of live audience shows planned for 2023, Q4. FWRD were tasked with building a centralised web platform that would house all key information, as well as supporting the event as a promotional tool.

The remedy With a clear idea of branding from the get-go (NYP’s classic 70s trope), building a platform to match the hyperbolic themes was the priority. Working with a host of stakeholders and talent, FWRD created custom illustrations, graphics and site elements to match the core concept, with a structure and layout to bring each page to life. All key information for each event was segmented into their own individual pages, including location, timings, talent and ticketing portal.

The result We’re pleased to have assisted in creating a web foundation for Name Your Price that is a complete reflection of clear, coherent and vibrant branding, that is also an opportunity to showcase content and scale further verticals.
